Our Story

Tracy and Tom's Travel Agency was founded in 1990 by Tracy and Tom. The company started as a small travel agency consulting firm, offering services to local residents in the area. Over the years, the company expanded its services and customer base, eventually becoming a leading provider of travel services

Founding values

  1. Family
  2. Affordable Prices
  3. Variety

Milestones

  • 1990

    Tracy and Tom's Travel Agency is founded by Tracy and Tom.

  • 1996

    Tracy and Tom's Travel Agency opens its first satellite office in a nearby city.

  • 2002

    Tracy and Tom's Travel Agency introduces its first Hot air ballon traveling product, which quickly becomes a best-seller in the industry.

  • 2005

    Tracy and Tom's Travel Agency launches its first mobile app, allowing customers to access its services on the go.

  • 2010

    Tracy and Tom's Travel Agency expands its services to international markets, opening offices in Europe and Asia.

  • 2015

    Tracy and Tom's Travel Agency celebrates its 25th anniversary and is recognized as a leading technology travel agency by industry publications.

  • 2020

    Tracy and Tom's Travel Agency pivots to focus on developing innovative solutions for local vacations in response to the pandemic
